# Studfinder goggles
These black and yellow Zircon-branded goggles replace normal vision with electroreception, painting the world in three colors: dielectric constant (conductivity), magnetic dipole (magnetic sensitivity), and electric field (charge or current). They also helpfully indicate any detected studs — structurally important elements, classically handsome muscular men, fast stallions, bulls whose mothers yielded a lot of milk, small post earrings that don't dangle, and so on — with a blinking arrow. The goggles sometimes resist having their area of central focus pointed away from studs.
