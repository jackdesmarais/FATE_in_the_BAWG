# IKEA price checker smart goggles
Originally designed to ease identification and purchase of IKEA items in showrooms, these stylish primary blue and yellow goggles have been retrofitted with an air reservoir, pump, and turbine for power. They use near-infrared lasers to identify items in your field of view, displaying the name, price, and description of the nearest IKEA equivalent. Since they use near-IR lasers rather than visible light, they helpfully operate in darkness — though they do not provide a visual image of the room, only item identification overlays, and they cannot see through magical darkness or obstacles.
