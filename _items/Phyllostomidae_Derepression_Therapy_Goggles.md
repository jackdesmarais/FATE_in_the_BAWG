# Phyllostomidae Derepression Therapy Goggles
An elaborate padded helmet featuring fold-down goggles, headphones, and straws for your mouth and nose. The back holds a compressed air reservoir with a hand pump. On top sits a wire cage with a roost and a mechanical arm with a suction cup. Originally designed for Phyllostomidae Derepression Therapy — a process of viewing your own deep memories by manifesting them as a bat — they also allow the wearer to access the senses of a bat at a slight delay. Useful for scouting if you don't mind being a little amnesiac while you do it.

## Aspects
**Enchanted pneumatic memory bat goggles** \
**Clanking and whirling** \
**My two enchantment cores barely tolerate each other** \
**I allow bat sight at a slight delay if you are ok being semi-amnesiac**

## Extras
**Kaajisik-soots:** The wearer can manifest their memories as a bat. While the bat is manifested they have no direct access to their own memories. They cannot directly control the bat but it will remember instructions thought of before manifesting. Attacks on the bat do mental stress to the wearer. The bat can only manifest or be absorbed on the roost in the cage.

**Soots-kaajisik:** The wearer can directly experience the memories of a bat roosted in the cage. While experiencing a memory they cannot experience the outside world.
