# Potion of visual information transfer
A vial of perfectly dark liquid that somehow manages to sound, smell, feel, and equilibropercept like the visual appearance of whatever is behind it. Drinking it causes instant and total blindness, but grants the ability to determine the same positional, color, and identity information about nearby objects as if one could still see them — using the other senses instead. The sensory field moves with the drinker. Best used when transitioning from a visually rich environment into somewhere where hearing and smell would be more useful than sight. This ability manifests as a consequence.

## Extras
**Adaptation:** The drinker can detect visual information through their other senses. This can be disorienting and opens the user up to opponents exploiting their equilibroperception using visual stimuli.
