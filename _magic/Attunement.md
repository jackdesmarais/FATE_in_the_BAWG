# Attunement
## Permissions
Have an aspect that reflects your status as a spirit with a connection to a Realm. (Only accessible for spirits, those with a bargain, those corrupted by channeling or with some other extenuating circumstance)

## Cost
Reduce your refresh by 1

## Description
You are attuned to a specific concept in how it interacts with the world. For each level you have spent on this ability you gain the ability to:
1. Sense
2. Strengthen/weaken
3. Control
4. Transmute/destroy
5. Redefine

You use normal skills for these actions, defaulting to Attune \[concept]. You can take one resonance to add a level of [Scale](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/subsystems/Scale.html) to these actions.

If the [spiritual realm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Spiritual_realms.html) you connect to hasn't been described yet, make it.
