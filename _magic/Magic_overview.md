# Magic Overview

Magic in the BAWG is built around the idea of **Spiritual realms** — vast conceptual forces (Bog, Karst, Full darkness, Molten Sugar, and many others) that permeate the world. People who develop connections to these realms can draw on their power in several ways.

## How magic works

There are four main ways a character can interact with a [Spiritual realm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Spiritual_realms.html):

- **[Channeling](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Channeling.html)** — Directly summoning the stuff of your realm into the world. The most common and least subtle form of magic.
- **[Adaptations](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Adaptations.html)** — Intrinsic physical abilities that arise from deep exposure to a realm. Things you can simply *do*.
- **[Attunement](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Attunement.html)** — A more refined spiritual connection allowing you to sense, control, transmute, or redefine aspects of your realm. Primarily accessible to spirits.
- **[Bargaining](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Bargaining.html)** — Trading a piece of your humanity to an avatar for a portion of their power.

## Costs and risks

Using magic at elevated [Scale](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/subsystems/Scale.html) or exposure to dense concentrations of magical energy builds up [Corruption](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Corruption.html) — excess [Resonance](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Resonance.html) that can mutate, curse, and ultimately transform a character into an [Avatar](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Avatars.html) or [Spirit](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Spirits.html).
