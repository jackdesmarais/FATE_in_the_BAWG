# Spirits
Spirits are semi-autonomous manifestations of [Spiritual realms](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Spiritual_realms.html). They arise when [Resonance](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Resonance.html) accumulates enough to develop a kind of consciousness. Unlike [Avatars](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Avatars.html), spirits are smaller-scale manifestations that represent specific sub-aspects or zones of a realm.

Sprites are a common form of spirit — tiny, human-shaped manifestations of specific forces, environments, or emotions. They can arise through [Bargaining](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Bargaining.html) when a resonance has absorbed enough pieces of human humanity to become self-sustaining.
