# Avatars
Avatars are large-scale manifestations of [Spiritual realms](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Spiritual_realms.html) — beings that embody and enact the truths of their realm in the physical world. They operate at a base [Scale](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/subsystems/Scale.html) of +2 or higher and are treated as a map with one zone per approach.

Characters who accumulate too much [Corruption](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Corruption.html) risk transforming into avatars — permanently crossing over into the realm they are connected to and becoming a bridge between it and the physical world.

Avatars can enter into [Bargaining](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Bargaining.html) arrangements with mortals, trading portions of their purview for pieces of humanity.

Known avatars include [Karst](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Karst.html).
