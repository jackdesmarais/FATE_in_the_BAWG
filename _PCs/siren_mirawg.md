# Siren Mirawg
A prominent DJ from [The Depths](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) who draws power from the realm of deception. She has a history of working with [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html), who once hooked her up with good DJ equipment in exchange for her DJing his daughter's bat mitzvah. She performs regularly at venues like [Cwahhhsahhhnt](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Cwahhhsahhhnt.html).
