# Session Notes
In our character question section, we learned that [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) once ordered wildly too much liquid smoke for making bacon and smoke flavored candy, and for a long while she was repeatedly launching smoke flavored candies to use it up. [Leow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html) once bought Theodosia (a past crush who lived nearby) flowers from a local shop and got her literally all the flowers from the shop. [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) once accidentally ordered very tiny wireframes for sculpture.

Today we had a battle! [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) fought the [Thunder (-sand) cat](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/creatures/Thunder_sand_cat.html) spraying it with mud that grounded its electricity. [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) blocked the [Thunder (-sand) cat's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/creatures/Thunder_sand_cat.html) lightning strike and finished tying up the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) with licorice. [Leow Wind](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html) fought through a stun from the [Thunder (-sand) cat's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/creatures/Thunder_sand_cat.html) thunder and struck back by grounding it with bent wet stone! [Diwmm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/Dimm_thDilf.html) struck it with his club accidentally grounding it and wounding it enough it was forced to flee. Then [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html) and [Raoul](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Raoul.html) arrived. They stalked into the same zone as the escaping BAWGies exposing them all to [Elif Onrall's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html) dark radiance. [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) was overcome by her rage and struck with an overwhelming blast of vines killing the tied up roof wardens but [Raoul](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Raoul.html) was able to defend himself and [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html) by vomiting waves of black fire that pushed, froze and corroded the vines, but was not able to avoid taking minor injuries. The others felt their darker urges rise but resisted and they all fled the warehouse.
