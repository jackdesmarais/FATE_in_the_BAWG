# Session Notes
Kicked off session with a quick intro of the rules to Jack Zuckerman and Felicia. Did a quick run of character creation with Jack Zuckerman and introduced his character [Diwmm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/Dimm_thDilf.html) from the great city of Detroit. Then we jumped into the session. We gave a long recap to introduce the new players to the setting and launched into the action in [Cwahhhsahhhnt](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Cwahhhsahhhnt.html). [Siren](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/siren_mirawg.html) started us off by DJing a set in the basement club. While this was happening [Leow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html), [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html), and [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) headed upstairs where they found [Diwmm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/Dimm_thDilf.html) losing at tiles to [Jed Huckleberry](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Jed_Huckleberry.html) at a table with [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html) and [Bernadett Sterkins](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Bernadett_Sterkins.html). He has made absolutely no concession to being able to see in the dark and has been losing because he can't see his tiles. [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) investigates the crew, getting the sense that [Jed Huckleberry](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Jed_Huckleberry.html) is cheating. [Leow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html) hears from [Karst](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Karst.html) that someone at the table has the resonance of hungry predatory darkness that they had sensed in the [Windy Gate Public Library](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Windy_Gate_Public_Library.html) and [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) was able to determine that it was [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html). When [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) approached to confront her she noticed that her anger about her treatment by her parents began to swell, and that [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html) mentioned that she could help her escape from the influence of those she is trying to escape in [The Bog City](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Bog_city.html) before she mentioned trying to escape something herself. They quickly wrap up their game, helping [Diwmm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/Dimm_thDilf.html) claw back his debts, and head downstairs. Meanwhile, [Siren](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/siren_mirawg.html) wraps up her set downstairs and goes up to the bar where she bumps into [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html), who she has worked with in the past hooking her up with good DJ equipment in exchange for having her DJ his daughter's bat mitzvah. He offers to get her into the state dinner in exchange for her DJing an after party he is hosting. She has a sense this means he is up to shenanigans but she is in for the ride. The rest of the party came downstairs to join them. They indicate that they have had some trouble with the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) and that [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) is associated with [The Bog Witch Delegation](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/The_Bog_Witch_Delegation.html). [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html) offers to help them hide out from the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) and to hook them up with night vision for [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) and [Diwmm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/Dimm_thDilf.html) in exchange for getting him some tickets to [The festivals of Ammothyella](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/The_festivals_of_Ammothyella.html) diplomatic dinner. We also learned that [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html)'s mom [The Bog Witch](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/The_Bog_Witch.html) is named [Morning Glowria Sundew](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Morning_Glowria_Sundew.html).
