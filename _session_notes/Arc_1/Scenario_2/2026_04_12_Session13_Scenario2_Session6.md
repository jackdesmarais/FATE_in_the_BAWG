# Session Notes
Much of this session was spent in discussion with [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html). They completed the goggle sale; he offered them [Studfinder goggles](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Studfinder_goggles.html), [IKEA price checker smart goggles](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/IKEA_price_checker_smart_goggles.html), [Phyllostomidae Derepression Therapy Goggles](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Phyllostomidae_Derepression_Therapy_Goggles.html), and a [Potion of visual information transfer](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Potion_of_visual_information_transfer.html). They selected the [Studfinder goggles](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Studfinder_goggles.html) for [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) and the [Phyllostomidae Derepression Therapy Goggles](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Phyllostomidae_Derepression_Therapy_Goggles.html) for [Diwmm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/Dimm_thDilf.html). They learned about the 3 Chunker families [House Owne](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Owne.html), [House Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Drell.html), and [House Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Grlenn.html) that are important in the politics of [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html). They learned that [House Owne](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Owne.html) dominates the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) and channels [Full darkness](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/spiritual_realms/Full_darkness.html), a realm of darkness imagined as not just actual darkness, but also fear, flaw, decay, and corrosion. This is likely what the group sensed on [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html). [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) gave him the low down on what they had discovered so far about the [Roof Warden](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) plot. He suggested that there are other people in [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) who don't like the current [Roof Warden](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) foreign policy, and he can bring them along to an event tonight to meet some of them. He also offered to help get them into the basement of [The Hall of the Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Hall_of_the_Roof_Wardens.html) if they want to try and heist [The Darudian Ritualis](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/The_Darudian_Ritualis.html) and the [Right Eye of Aeolenon](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Right_Eye_of_Aeolenon.html). They decided to go with him to the event and possibly try the heist later. As they went to leave, they noticed [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html) and [Raoul](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Raoul.html) in a patch of darkness watching the door for them to leave. They were able to hurry downstairs losing their tail with the help of a lot of stealth and the [Reverse Sole Pneumatic Radio Shoes](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Reverse_Sole_Pneumatic_Radio_Shoes.html). [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html) guided them through the backstage of the club out to an alley and hustled through the night to [Maggie's Marvels](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Maggies_Marvels.html).
