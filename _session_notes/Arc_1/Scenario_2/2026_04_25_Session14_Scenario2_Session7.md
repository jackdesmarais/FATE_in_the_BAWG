# Session Notes
We learned about [The crystal listeners](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/The_crystal_listeners.html), [The legend of the candy factory](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/The_legend_of_the_candy_factory.html), and [The poison ivies](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/The_poison_ivies.html) in our character question section. The group arrived at [Maggie's Marvels](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Maggies_Marvels.html), and [Leow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html) quickly realized that he had no memory of how they had gotten there other than starting to feel that their path was curving back on itself in impossible ways. [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html) introduced them to [Magda Morgantha](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) and [Perihild Longstrider](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Perihild_Longstrider.html). [Leow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html), [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html), and [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) made their pitches to [Maggie](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) that she should trust them and got her onside. While this was happening, [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) noticed that [Maggie](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) and [Peri](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Perihild_Longstrider.html) were avoiding certain parts of the shop while walking around and that they seemed to be occasionally consulting the empty air, but neither she nor [Leow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html) could determine what was going on. They found out that in addition to being organizers of the [Chthonian Peace Faction](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Chthonian_Peace_Faction_semi_militarized.html), [Maggie](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) and [Peri](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Perihild_Longstrider.html) are witches who sell resonance from their shop for the crafting of items and potions, the forging of links to new realms, and manifesting sprites. [Maggie](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) channels [Mutual information](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/spiritual_realms/Mutual_information.html), which she uses to perform tarot and tea leaf readings. [Peri](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Perihild_Longstrider.html) channels [Curvature](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/spiritual_realms/Curvature.html), which is useful for moving quickly and looking around corners (and up nostrils). [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) gets [Maggie](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) to promise her a reading. They then head out of the shop, immediately losing any sense of the path they took before ending up at the [Chthonian Peace Faction Warehouse](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Chthonian_peace_faction_warehouse.html). There they meet [Hakan Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Hakan_Grlenn.html) and [Gulcan Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Gulcan_Drell.html). [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) bribes them with candy, and they discuss strategies for overcoming the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html). [Hakan Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Hakan_Grlenn.html) wants to cut off [Aeol's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html) water now while they still have leverage. [Gulcan Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Gulcan_Drell.html) wants to try to build a surface colony to get their own access to food. [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) tells them who she is and that her plan is to steal [The Darudian Ritualis](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/The_Darudian_Ritualis.html) and the [Right Eye of Aeolenon](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/items/Right_Eye_of_Aeolenon.html) from the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) and reveal at the dinner that they saved the city — forcing the [Aeol City Government](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Aeol_City_Government.html) to continue helping [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) or lose face. While this was happening, [Maggie](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) was doing a reading and announced that their cover had been blown and the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) were here. [Maggie](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) and [Peri](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Perihild_Longstrider.html) warped out of the room. [Lawrel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html) found the exits. Three [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) and a [Thunder (-sand) cat](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/creatures/Thunder_sand_cat.html) breached a loading bay door of the [warehouse](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Chthonian_peace_faction_warehouse.html). [Taffy](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) gummed up the hands of the attacking wardens and saw that the [Thunder (-sand) cat](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/creatures/Thunder_sand_cat.html) was electrically charged. [Leow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/leow_wind.html) saw that the [Thunder (-sand) cat](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/creatures/Thunder_sand_cat.html) was floating above the ground and watched it reform after being blown apart. [Gulcan Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Gulcan_Drell.html), [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html), and [Hakan Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Hakan_Grlenn.html) ran for different exits. We were also reminded that guessing someone's aspect lets you get a free invoke on it.
