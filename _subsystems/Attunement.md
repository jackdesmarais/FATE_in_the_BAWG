# Attunement
## Permissions
Have an aspect that reflects your status as a spirit with a connection to a Realm. (Only accessible for spirits or those with a bargain or other extenuating circumstance)
## Cost
Reduce your refresh by 1
A skill slot
## Description
You are attuned to a specific concept in how it interacts with the world. For each level you have spent on this ability you gain the ability to
1. Sense
2. Strengthen/weaken
3. Control
4. Transmute/destroy
5. Redefine
You use normal skills for these actions. Defaulting to Attune \[concept].
You can take one resonance to add a level of [Scale](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/subsystems/Scale.html) to these actions.

If the [spiritual realm](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/subsystems/Spiritual_realms.html) you connect to hasn't been described yet make it.

### Inspiration
Mage the Awakening
GURPS Realm magic
