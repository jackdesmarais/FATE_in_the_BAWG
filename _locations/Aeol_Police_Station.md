# Aeol Police Station
Taking up the two floors about 30 stories up in one of [The Spires](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/the_spires.html) in [Aeol](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html). It has a large waiting room, with a desk sergeant, and a bull pen of beat cops and detectives surrounded by offices of high ranking officials. The corner office is occupied by [Police Commissioner Denise Demeric](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Police_Commissioner_Denise_Demeric.html).
