# Maggie's Marvels
A small rough hewn cavern chipped out of the side of the passageway. A tall thin woman in floor length black scaled leather coat, and pointy wide brimmed hat sits behind the counter operating a complicated resonance distilling apparatus. She is wearing at least 4 scarves, a mix of knit, leather, metalic, and normal fabric. Her arms are dripping with a variety of bangles. A shorter woman wearing leather and wool traveling gear and a floppy felt pointy hat with wide brim emerges from the back room. As they move around the shop, you may notice that they sometimes walk in slightly odd patterns as if they are avoiding walking into something you can't see.

The walls of her shop are filled with tiny bottles, each of which seems to swirl with a different substance, darkness, light, sand, a ship foundering in a storm, swirling runes, tiny forested clearings, and some which at first glance seem empty but fill you with longing, or fear or the sense of excitement when a friend is about to whisper a secret into your ear. Flitting around the store are a variety of tiny sprites - human shaped manifestations of various forces, environments, and emotions.
