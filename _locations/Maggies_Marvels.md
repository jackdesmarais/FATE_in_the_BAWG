# Maggie's Marvels
A small rough hewn cavern chipped out of the side of the passageway in [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html). Outside hangs a gas jet neon sign with a witch's hat propped jauntily on a glass bottle. you can't remember how you got here. You remember walking, taking endless turns many of which seem nonsensical, turning right from a market street to find yourself entering the market from the opposite direction, walking around a fountain to find yourself back where you started but with the fountain on the opposite side. Finally, you found yourself in an abandoned passageway with the sign hanging before you and no memory of turning onto the passage or what street it connects too. Inside, [Magda Morgantha](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) sits behind the counter in a floor length black scaled leather coat and pointy wide brimmed hat, operating a complicated resonance distilling apparatus. [Perihild Longstrider](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Perihild_Longstrider.html) emerges from the back room in wool and leather traveling gear and a floppy felt pointy hat. As they move around the shop, you may notice that they sometimes walk in slightly odd patterns as if they are avoiding walking into something you can't see.

The walls of the shop are filled with tiny bottles, each of which seems to swirl with a different substance — darkness, light, sand, a ship foundering in a storm, swirling runes, tiny forested clearings — and some which at first glance seem empty but fill you with longing, fear, or the sense of excitement when a friend is about to whisper a secret into your ear. Flitting around the store are a variety of tiny sprites: human shaped manifestations of various forces, environments, and emotions.
