# Siroccow Palace
This towering spire is entirely dedicated to use as the residence and seat of government for [Orsow Siroccow](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Orsow_Siroccow.html), Grand Duke of [Aeol](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html) and leader of the [Aeol City Government](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Aeol_City_Government.html).
