# Coco Cove
A coastal town famous for its candy making. [Taffy Tawffee's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/taffy_tawffee.html) family is from Coco Cove, and the town hosts the [Big Candy Expo](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/Big_Candy_Expo.html) every autumn. It is also the home of [The legend of the candy factory](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/The_legend_of_the_candy_factory.html).
