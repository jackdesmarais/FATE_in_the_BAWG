# Chthonian Peace Faction Warehouse
An out-of-the-way storage cavern in an industrial part of [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) used by the [Chthonian Peace Faction](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Chthonian_Peace_Faction_semi_militarized.html) for meetings. Loading bay doors are present on one wall and possible hidden doors on two other walls.

This is where the party met [Hakan Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Hakan_Grlenn.html) and [Gulcan Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Gulcan_Drell.html), debated water-embargo vs. surface-colony strategies against [House Owne](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Owne.html) and the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html), and were ambushed by a [Roof Warden](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) raid with a [Thunder (-sand) cat](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/creatures/Thunder_sand_cat.html).

## Aspects
**Industrial-district storage cavern** \
**Hidden behind curved paths** \
**Loading bays on every wall**
