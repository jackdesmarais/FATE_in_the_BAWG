# Aeol City Hall
The bureaucratic seat of the [Aeol City Government](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Aeol_City_Government.html). During the Fall [The festivals of Ammothyella](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/The_festivals_of_Ammothyella.html), the official agreement between [Aeol](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html) and [The Depths](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) is signed here.
