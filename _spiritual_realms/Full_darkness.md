# Full darkness
A realm of not just literal darkness, but also fear, flaw, decay, and corrosion — imagined as the final state of the universe. Full darkness breaks the bonds of electromagnetism, hides things from sight, and brings dread. It is heavily associated with [House Owne](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Owne.html) and the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html). [Elif Onrall](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Elif_Onrall.html) is a known channeler of this realm.

## Truths
1. **Full Darkness** is the absence of light
2. **Full Darkness** breaks the bonds of electro-magnetism
3. In **Full Darkness** heroes are flawed
4. **Full Darkness** hides things from sight
5. **Full Darkness** brings dread
6. **Full Darkness** is the final state of the universe
