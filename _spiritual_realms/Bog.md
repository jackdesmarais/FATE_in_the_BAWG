# Bog
The realm channeled by [Lawrel Sundew](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html).

# Truths
1. The bog is the flora, fauna, and fungi that create it 
2. The bog maintains its ecosystem 
3. The bog is dirty 
4. The bog grows life from death 
5. The bog never rests 
6. The bog brings order to chaos and chaos to order
