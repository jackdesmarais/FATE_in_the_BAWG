# Mutual information
The realm of correlations, signals, and what one thing reveals about another. Channelers of Mutual information cause seemingly random events to shed light on what they are interested in — making it a powerful realm for divination, tarot, and tea leaf readings. [Magda Morgantha](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) is a known channeler.
