# Secrets
The realm of hidden knowledge, obfuscation, and the power that flows from what is concealed.

## Truths
1. **Secrets** are facts only a few people know
2. **Secrets** are actively hidden by those who know them
3. The dead keep their **Secrets**
4. When **Secrets** come to light the powerful fall
5. **Secrets** have a way of getting out
6. Shared **Secrets** bring connection and control

## Approaches
1. Secretly
2. Obfuscationaly
3. Necromanticly
4. Implosively
5. Slipperily
6. Inextricably

## Channeling examples
1. Create an advantage
	1. Make information secret by removing it from public knowledge
	2. Make a secret more damaging
	3. Make people more motivated to suppress a secret
2. Overcome
	1. Reduce the fallout of a leaked secret by making it secret again
3. Attack
	1. Deal reputation stress by making your deeds secrets, making existing secrets get out, or making existing secrets more damaging
4. Defend
	1. Defend against reputation attacks by making rumors into secrets

## Adaptations
1. You can use a ouija board to ask the dead questions about their secrets if you have a piece of the person. They don't have to answer, so you may have to bargain.
2. When you have shared a secret with someone you are always considered to be in physical proximity with them. This goes both ways.
3. You may compel someone to answer when asking specific questions about their secrets. Count this as an attack against their mental stress if they resist answering.

## Attunement
1. **Sense:** You can detect when someone has a secret, and track a specific secret you know about.
2. **Control:** Move knowledge of a secret from one person to another, or move a secret between a book and someone's mind.
3. **Transmute:** Change which secret is encoded in a location.
4. **Redefine:** Secrets are transmissible like diseases, each with a different transmission mechanism.
