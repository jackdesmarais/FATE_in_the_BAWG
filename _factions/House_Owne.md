# House Owne
One of the three major Chunker families of [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html). House Owne currently dominates the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html) and channels [Full darkness](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/spiritual_realms/Full_darkness.html) — a realm of not just literal darkness, but also fear, flaw, decay, and corrosion. [Jeref Owne](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Jeref_Owne.html) is a prominent member.
