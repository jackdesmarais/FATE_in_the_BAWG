# Chthonian Peace Faction (semi-millitarized)
A group of [Chthonians](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) who oppose the current foreign policy of [House Owne](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Owne.html) and the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html). They meet in an out-of-the-way storage cavern in an industrial part of [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) to work on ways of breaking [House Owne's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Owne.html) hold on the Chthonian foreign ministry and on building a Chthonian surface colony.

The faction is organized by the witches [Magda Morgantha](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html) and [Perihild Longstrider](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Perihild_Longstrider.html), and includes minor children of [House Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Grlenn.html) and [House Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Drell.html) — notably [Hakan Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Hakan_Grlenn.html) and [Gulcan Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Gulcan_Drell.html) — who lead competing sub-factions. [Sed Strongsteel](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Sed_Strongsteel.html) helps the group acquire goods and information they couldn't otherwise reach.

There is internal debate over strategy: [House Grlenn's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Grlenn.html) preferred approach is an immediate water embargo against [Aeol](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html), while [House Drell's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Drell.html) preferred approach is the secret surface colony.
