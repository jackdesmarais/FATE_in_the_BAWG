# The poison ivies
A secret society that upholds the environmental code of the bog. They protect the [Bog](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/spiritual_realms/Bog.html) and help revert old buildings back to nature. Many members are partially [avatar](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/magic/Avatars.html) transformed — like Diana's hunters, they take up the mantle and give up their humanity to join.
