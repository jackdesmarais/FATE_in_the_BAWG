# Sed Strongsteel
A gregarious smuggler from [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) with a knack for moving things through channels others can't access. He knows the hidden ways of transporting things through the depths and has connections throughout [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) and [Aeol](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html).
