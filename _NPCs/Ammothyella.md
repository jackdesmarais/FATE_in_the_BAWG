# Ammothyella
A legendary avatar of glitterstorms. An existential threat featured in [The legend of Aeolenon](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/The_legend_of_Aeolenon.html). Its defeat is celebrated in [The festivals of Ammothyella](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/culture/The_festivals_of_Ammothyella.html).
