# Raoul
A body guard associated with the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html). When above ground wears a full length trench coat, hood, mask and glasses. Shaved head, very pale skin and blown out pupils. When his mouth is open, you can see that his teeth are red tinted and nothing is visible behind them. As he readies for a fight you see the light around him red shift and cold flames of redshift and darkness bleed out from his mouth bursting and corroding what they touch.
