# Hakan Grlenn
A muscular man in a golf shirt and khakis. A minor child of [House Grlenn](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Grlenn.html) who leads a sub-faction within the [Chthonian Peace Faction](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Chthonian_Peace_Faction.html). He favors cutting off [Aeol's](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html) water immediately while [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) still has leverage.
