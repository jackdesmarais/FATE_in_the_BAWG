# Perihild Longstrider
Also known as Peri. A woman of average height (and somewhat greater than average curvature) who wears wool and leather traveling gear and a felted pointy wide brimmed hat. She carries a whittled wood staff, and when she isn't wearing her battered leather frame pack you can see the wear patterns it has left on her shirt. She works out of [Maggie's Marvels](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Maggies_Marvels.html) with [Magda Morgantha](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/NPCs/Magda_Morgantha.html), and the two of them organize the [Chthonian Peace Faction](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Chthonian_Peace_Faction_semi_militarized.html).

She is an expert at channeling [Curvature](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/spiritual_realms/Curvature.html), which lets her bend space to walk long distances, slip free of bonds, slip around barriers, see around corners, and alter gravity.
