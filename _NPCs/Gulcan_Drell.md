# Gulcan Drell
A thin woman in a business suit. A minor child of [House Drell](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/House_Drell.html) who leads a sub-faction within the [Chthonian Peace Faction](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Chthonian_Peace_Faction_semi_militarized.html). She favors building a secret surface colony so [Chthonia](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/The_Depths.html) can secure its own access to food.
