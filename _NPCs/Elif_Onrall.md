# Elif Onrall
A spy and provocateur for the [Roof Wardens](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/factions/Roof_Wardens.html). A thin pale woman with dark hair. She wears a dark leather coat and pants. She has a high undercut on one side and a length of thin chain helixed through that ear underneath a set of 12 rings. One eye is a crazed sphere of non-reflective black glass; the cracks in the eye spread into the shaved side of her head, where she appears to be made entirely of non-reflective black glass. She channels [Full darkness](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/spiritual_realms/Full_darkness.html).
