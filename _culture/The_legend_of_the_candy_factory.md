# The legend of the candy factory
A legend in [Coco Cove](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Coco_Cove.html). In the old days, every year ten people were invited to the factory. Now the factory is closed and haunted because of all the deaths, and there are lots of conspiracy theories about what it used to be like and what actually happened. Golden tickets are now a prank people play to send each other to the haunted factory.
