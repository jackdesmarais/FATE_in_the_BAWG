# The crystal listeners
A conspiracy theory in [Aeol](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/locations/Aeol.html) that the government has developed tech to listen to noise in the crystals and decode conversations and movements — functionally bugging the entire city. Few people actually believe it, but parents tell it to their kids so they behave.
