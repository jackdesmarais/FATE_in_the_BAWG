# The great bubbling
The great bubbling happens at the beginning of spring, when the frost thaws and the first big gas bubble breaks free. There is a bubble popping competition for the kids. [Lawrel Sundew](/FATE_in_the_BAWG/PCs/lawrel_sundew.html)'s favorite holiday.
